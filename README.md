# 🎮 Life RPG – Gamify Your Life

> What if your life worked like a game?

**Life RPG** is a Python-based terminal application that transforms your daily actions into experience points (XP), helping you level up your real life like a character in a game.


---

## 💡 Concept
I've always struggled with consistency and felt,what if my life was a game? and I saw a reel on instagram which bought forth this idea,but it was a paid tracker,so it made me think,I'm an engineer with a free will,so why not make one by myself?,and here we are.

Instead of boring habit tracking, this app lets you:
- 🎯 Assign XP to real-life actions
- 📜 Log your daily activities
- ⚡ Gain XP and level up
- 🔥 Build streaks through consistency

Turn your life into something you *want* to improve.

---

## ⚙️ Features

- 🧠 **Custom Action System**  
  Save actions like "drink water", "study", "workout" with fixed XP  

- 📜 **Daily Logging**  
  Log actions and track what you’ve done  

- ⚡ **XP & Level System**  
  Gain XP and level up every 100 points  

- 🔥 **Streak Tracker**  
  Stay consistent and build daily streaks  

- 💾 **Persistent Storage**  
  Saves your progress using JSON
